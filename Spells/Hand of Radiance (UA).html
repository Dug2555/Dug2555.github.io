Hand of Radiance (UA)

Level: 0

Classes: Cleric

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 5 feet

Duration: Instantaneous

Components: V S

You raise your hand, and burning radiance erupts from it. Each creature of your choice that you can see within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 radiant damage. At Higher Levels: The spell's damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d8).
