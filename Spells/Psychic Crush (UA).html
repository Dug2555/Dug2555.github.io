Psychic Crush (UA)

Level: 6

Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

School: Enchantment

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60 Feet

Duration: 1 minute

Components: V S

You overload the mind of one creature you can see within range, filling its psyche with discordant emotions. The target must make an Intelligence saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 12d6 psychic damage and is stunned for 1 minute. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage and isn't stunned. The stunned target can make an Intelligence saving throw at the end of each of its turns. On a successful save, the spell ends on the target.
